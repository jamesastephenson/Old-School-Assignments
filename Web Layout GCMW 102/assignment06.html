Shrimp Tempura

Original Recipe

Ingredients

  • 10 Prawns
  • Corn Starch (for dusting)
  • 3 Cups Vegetable Oil
  • 1 Large Egg
  • 3/4 Cup Iced Water
  • 1 Cup All Purpose Flour

Directions

  1. Shell and de-vein the prawns. Make small horizontal cuts so that the prawns can be laid straight.
  2. Heat the vegetable oil to 340 degrees Fahrenheit in a high-walled pot.
  3. Sift the flour into a large bowl.
  4. Combine egg and iced water in a measuring cup. Slowly stir into the bowl of flour until the batter reaches a lumpy consistency.
  5. Coat the shrimp in the batter, make sure to fully submerge for best results.
  6. Deep fry the coated shrimp in the pot of oil until golden brown (about 2 minutes). Be careful not to crowd the pot because the oil temperature will drop quickly. Remember to be careful when deep frying.
  7. When frying is complete, take out the prawns and let them dry over paper towels before serving.
