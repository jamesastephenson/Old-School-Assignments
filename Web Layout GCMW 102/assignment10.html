New Town Resources

Community Development:
We have built a new recreation center on Fall Street, which we hope all citizens will take advantage of. It will include several gyms, pools, and art classrooms to help enrich our community.

Refuse and Recycling:
We are proud to announce our new recycling initiative, estimated to reduce the town's carbon footprint by 60%. A new recycling center is planned to be completed on June 20th in Greenbury Grove.


James Stephenson, Palomar College 2021
